About Us

.

Founded in 2018, FootLockRE is a dynamic Indonesian multinational footwear company that has rapidly become a trendsetter in the global footwear industry. Our journey began in our third year with our first store in the famous Grand Indonesia Mall and since then we have expanded our reach around the world. With offices in strategic countries, we have become the go-to destination for fashion-conscious individuals looking for innovative and stylish footwear. At FootLockRE, we strive to stay ahead of the curve by constantly developing and exploring new footwear trends. Our team of talented designers combine creativity, craftsmanship and a keen eye for global fashion to create shoes that not only set trends, but exceed expectations in quality and comfort. Whether it's elegant sneakers, elegant suit shoes or casual sandals, our diverse product line offers the perfect fit for every style and occasion. Join us on this exciting journey as we redefine footwear trends and help people around the world prove themselves with every step.

Style Meet Comfort

.

At FootLockRE, we believe style should never come at the expense of comfort. As an Indonesian multinational footwear company with offices around the world, we are dedicated to developing and researching new trends in the footwear industry. Our mission is to create shoes that seamlessly blend style and comfort so you don't have to compromise on either. With a passion for innovation and an unwavering commitment to quality, we strive to push the boundaries of fashion and deliver shoes that not only look great, but feel great. Join us on a journey where style meets comfort, and experience a world where your feet are stylish yet incredibly comfortable.
